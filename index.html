Permanent Change of Station

PCS is the official relocation of an active duty military service member – along with any family members living with him or her – to a different duty location, such as a military base.It is known to be a stressful time for a military family.

Tips to lighten PCS

  1. Create a personal moving calendar with checklists, phone numbers and links to critical moving processes and information
  2. Your Transportation Office (TO) or Personal Property Shipping Office (PPSO) is the primary point of contact for customer service. It is never too early to ask questions
  3. Once you get your orders and know the dates you want to move, immediately start the moving process. Contact your TO/PPSO to learn all options available to you, including a Personally Procured Move. The sooner you start the better chance you have to lock in your preferred move date.
